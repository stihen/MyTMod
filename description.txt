MyTMod is a mod for Terraria. As of now it's just a hobby project intended for personal use only.

Adds the following items to the game:

Melee Weapons:
	- Radiant Sword

Magic Weapons
	- Wind Tome
	- Wind Storm
	- Solar Rain (TODO)

Accessories
	- Beetle Shield
	- Beetle Aegis (Thorium)


Future:
- I'll probably add more items in the future as I've got some ideas.
